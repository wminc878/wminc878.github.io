About Exegesis

This is how you do it

Take classes and read books. Guthrie and Duval have a great book that teaches how to diagram and exegete the Greek New Testament. 

Diagramming the original language is a hugely helpful tool for getting into interpretation and figuring out what the text means.

Diagramming the greek text can be done in a more structural grammatical way, and also in a more semantic, interpretational way.
